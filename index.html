We have a solution for you - Codebase Health Check. What is Codebase Health Check?

Codebase Health Check is a detailed code quality report of your entire Ruby on Rails application, created just for you.

Why do I need a code quality report?

People don't discuss clean code practices merely for the sake of aesthetics. Code is written to deliver a business value. Low quality code slows down future growth. It negatively impacts your ability to implement new features. It increases the cost of maintaining the software. In short, a bad codebase costs you a lot of money!

On the other hand, it's really hard to think about this wider perspective when new features are what matter most right now. Fortunately, there are external experts who can evaluate your code's health for you.

Codebase Health Check is your unique chance to get a second opinion on the quality of your code. It's a fresh look from the perspective of someone who wasn't involved in writing the original code. This report allows you to determine the code quality in your project. It contains practical tips and suggestions on what should be improved.

What will be covered?

We will cover things like:

  • architecture of the application
  • adherence to best programming practices
  • division into smaller components
  • application of design patterns
  • gotchas which can bite you
  • potential performance bottlenecks
  • selection of dependencies

We won't report on minor issues like inconsistent indentation, the use of old Ruby syntax or unnecessary parenthesis. That's what code linters are for. Instead, we focus on things which cannot be automated and offer the condensed knowledge about building higher quality web applications.

How it works?

  1. You fill in the form.
  2. We contact you.
  3. You give us access to read the code.
  4. We deliver the report.

Codebase Health Check is for Ruby on Rails applications. This is what we are best at and want to focus on.
